Kimetsu No Yaiba

Demon Slayer refers to a popular Japanese manga and anime franchise titled Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba.

It follows Tanjiro Kamado, who joins the Demon Slayer Corps to avenge his family and cure his sister, who was turned into a demon.

The series is known for its animated visuals, exciting fight scenes, and compelling story, and includes a manga, a TV anime series, and several successful films, with a continued movie trilogy planned.
