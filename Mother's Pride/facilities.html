FACILITIES AT MOTHER'S PRIDE

AT MOTHER’S PRIDE PATAUDI, WE FEEL THAT ALL CHILDREN, REGARDLESS OF THEIR CULTURAL BACKGROUND, SHOULD HAVE THE RIGHT TO A PROPER AND DECENT ENVIRONMENT THAT IS CONDUCIVE TO THEIR GROWTH. IT IS FOR THIS REASON THAT OUR FACILITIES INCLUDE EVERY POSSIBLE RESOURCE THAT WOULD ASSIST A CHILD’S POTENTIAL TO FLOURISH IN EVERY WAY. HERE IS HOW WE STAND OUT FROM OTHER CBSE SCHOOLS IN PATAUDI, GURGAON, OR PRE SCHOOL IN PATAUDI, GURGAON:

Child Friendly Infrastructure

It is our belief that the infrastructure of our entire campus is a happy place, which ensures that children have the full freedom to move around and interact with their buddies and also different components of the world outside. Our campus’s architecture and layout inspire self-serve creativity and exploration due to its frinedly nature. It is thoughtfully furnished with colorful and spacious classrooms and other interactive areas that have been designed with children’s needs in mind. Developmentally, safety is also taken into consideration since the furniture is designed for children and the walls and floor patterns are also sensory friendly. From the children's playground zones to the calm zones, all elements are designed in a way to stimulate joy and learning in every corner of the campus.

Key Features of Our Infrastructure:

  • Right air quality and lighting improve overall learning.
  • To ensure a feeling of safety, corridors are obstacle-free, which allows students to be monitored 24/7 when they walk around.
  • Attention given to sound acoustics can reduce distractions during learning and also increase focus.
  • The bright colors and images stimulate students’ desire to create and work together.

Play Zone

Play is the answer to how anything new comes about!’ Playgrounds are places where the children enjoy the freedom to imagine, learn and explore the world at their own pace. Our colourful play area has been designed keeping in mind all the safety measures. Special emphasis has been laid on the play courses which allow the toddlers to run, jump, hide and enjoy the outdoors. The swings,slides, see-saw and other fun toys also enhance the gross and fine motor skills of the little ones.

Library

Step into our library at Mother’s Pride School, Pataudi, where the pages come alive, and knowledge knows no bounds. Join us in cultivating a community of lifelong learners, where the love for reading becomes the cornerstone of academic success and personal enrichment. Here, dive into the world of knowledge, a sanctuary for avid readers and scholars. A vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources awaits exploration. This space encourages independent research, supports academic endeavors, and provides a tranquil haven for intellectual pursuits.
